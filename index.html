Italy

Italy is my favourite country in Europe. I've been thethree times and every time I visit I fall in love with the country again. I'll share why I love the food and the scenery in the next paragraph.

Food and Scenery

Italian food is one of the most simple cuisine's I've had the pleasure of trying. What get's me excited about it is the simplicty and the burst of flavours. The Italians are very particular about how their food tastes and how it looks. You are always certian to get an instagram worthy meal at every restaurant. Here's my top three restuarants in Florence text here.

  • Trattoria Da Burde Firenze
  • Essenzia
  • Trattoria Mario

For me the Italian landscape and scenery is unmatched. The views of the mediterrean sea as you eat at a quaint restaurant can't be recreated anywhere else.
